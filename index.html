About Us

Rikito Co. Ltd is a privately owned South Sudan-based company established in 2018. We have built a reputation for delivering quality projects on time, serving governments, NGOs, and private clients.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission is to provide exceptional construction and supply services that exceed client expectations, contribute to national development, and adhere to the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision is to be the most trusted and sought-after contractor in South Sudan.

Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment (QHSE)

We are deeply committed to creating a safe working environment and minimizing our ecological footprint. Every project we undertake is executed with a focus on superior materials, rigorous safety protocols, and responsible waste management.
